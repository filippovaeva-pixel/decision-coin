# Decision Coin

A coin flip for when you can't choose between two things — except right before it lands, it asks what you're actually hoping for.

## Live link

https://filippovaeva-pixel.github.io/decision-coin/

## How to use it

1. Open the link above.
2. Type your two options and click "Flip the coin."
3. Answer the gut-check question, or skip straight to the flip.
4. Watch the coin land — that's your answer.

## Known limitations

- The flip is genuinely 50/50 random.
- Only handles two options at a time.
- Doesn't save past decisions between visits.
